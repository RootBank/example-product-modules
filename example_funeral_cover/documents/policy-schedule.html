How to claim for a life covered

Should you or your beneficiary wish to initiate a claim you log into your app or reached out us on exampleinsurance.com or by contacting us. Once you have logged your claim, we will be in touch to update and guide you through the process.

Please make sure that you submit your claim within 90 days from the date of the occurrence of any event that could result in a claim against this policy. You can lodge a claim on our website or by calling us on 001-337-1337.
